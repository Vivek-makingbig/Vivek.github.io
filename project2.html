Stationery Business Project

For my entrepreneurship project, I initiated a Stationery Items Business. The aim was to provide essential school and office supplies to students and professionals, ensuring high-quality products at affordable prices.

Key Features:

  • Product Range: A wide selection of stationery items, including pens, notebooks, and art supplies.
  • Target Audience: Focused on students, teachers, and office professionals looking for reliable, quality stationery.
  • Affordable Pricing: Competitive pricing strategies to cater to budget-conscious buyers.
  • Sales and Marketing: Leveraged both online platforms and campus events to promote and sell products.

Items Sold:

  • Pens, pencils, and markers
  • Notebooks, diaries, and planners
  • Highlighters, sticky notes, and folders
  • Art supplies like sketchbooks and brushes

This project enhanced my skills in business planning, inventory management, customer interaction, and marketing. I also learned to adapt quickly to market trends and customer needs.
